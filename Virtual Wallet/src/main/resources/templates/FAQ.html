Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about FlowPay.

How do I create an account?
You can create an account by clicking the Register button and filling out your details.
What currencies does FlowPay support?
FlowPay supports multiple currencies, including USD, EUR, GBP, and more.
Is FlowPay secure for online transactions?
Yes, we use encryption and secure payment gateways to protect your transactions.
How long do transactions take?
Most transactions are processed instantly, but some may take up to 24 hours depending on your bank.
How can I contact FlowPay support?
You can contact our support team via email at support@flowpay.com or through our Contact page.
